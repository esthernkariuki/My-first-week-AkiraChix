Onboard process

I remember that day vividly since we were all excited being admitted here. It was quick since the institution had planned everything, hence making everything to be in order.

Being among the students learning in the tech industry is a great privilege. We were accompanied by our parents and since the institution is more advanced, they had handled everything making it precise. The institution is attractive, making us as students proud of it.

Good experiences

  1. They provide everything to every student.
  2. They try to make the student satisfied in every way such that they never lack things like toilets, food, and security.
  3. They provide care for every student by ensuring everyone is in good condition.
  4. They ensure we get to be trained by the best trainers in the whole world.

Bad experiences

  • Washing and scrubbing everything in the compound, lots of piled assignments.
  • A lot of food we never had before.
  • Trying to adapt to them.

Conclusion

By the end of the program, I am looking forward to being among the graduates and having a long life experience.

This will motivate me and continue supporting the institution to equip more ladies in the world of tech, since it is a diversified institution.
